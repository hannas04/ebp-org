Our Story

Born from a Love
of Celebration

Events by Pejublack was founded on one belief — that every occasion, no matter the size, deserves to be extraordinary. What began as a personal passion for creating beautiful, meaningful gatherings has grown into Lagos' most trusted full-service event planning and management company.

From our very first event, we set out to do things differently. We don't take on events; we take on dreams. We sit with our clients, learn their stories, understand what makes their celebration uniquely theirs, and then pour every ounce of our creativity and professionalism into bringing that vision to life.

Over the years, we have had the honour of planning weddings, corporate galas, milestone birthdays, intimate celebrations, and memorial tributes — each one handled with the same level of care, precision, and love. Our growth has been built entirely on referrals and repeat clients, a testament to the experiences we consistently deliver.

We don't just plan events — we craft experiences that live forever in the hearts of those who attend.

Meet the CEO

Peju Black —
The Vision Behind
Every Event

Oyepeju Ojikutu, known widely as Peju Black, is the founder and driving force behind Events by Pejublack. With over a decade of immersive experience in event design, planning, and management, Peju has built a reputation that is synonymous with elegance, precision, and genuine care for every client she serves.

Her journey into event planning was not accidental — it grew from a deep love for people and a natural instinct for creating environments where memories are made. She trained as a Certified Show Producer at the Mahogany Training Academy in Lagos, formalising the skills she had already been sharpening in the field for years.

What makes Peju exceptional is her ability to listen. She does not impose a style — she discovers yours, amplifies it, and delivers it back to you in a celebration that feels unmistakably, beautifully yours. From the first consultation to the final dance of the evening, she is present, meticulous, and tireless.

Her clients often say the same thing: they didn't just hire a planner, they found a guardian angel for the most important day of their lives.

What Drives Us

Our Goals & Objectives

Every decision we make, every team we build, every event we take on is guided by a clear sense of purpose — to set the standard for excellence in event planning across Africa and beyond.

Our Vision

  • To become Africa's most referenced luxury event company, known for turning ordinary occasions into extraordinary lifelong memories.
  • To expand our footprint across Nigeria, the UK, USA, and Canada — serving the Nigerian diaspora with the same warmth and excellence they receive at home.
  • To be the go-to name when clients say, "I want it done perfectly."

Our Mission

  • To deliver full-service event experiences that are deeply personal, beautifully executed, and consistently exceed client expectations.
  • To maintain absolute transparency with clients at every stage of the planning process — no surprises, no shortcuts.
  • To build a team culture where creativity, accountability, and care for the client are non-negotiable.

Our Key Objectives

  • Deliver every event on time, within budget, and above expectation — zero exceptions.
  • Maintain a 5-star client satisfaction rating across all review platforms through relentless quality.
  • Grow our certified team of event designers and coordinators to handle simultaneous large-scale events.
  • Deepen vendor relationships across Lagos to secure the best pricing and priority access for our clients.
  • Develop signature event concepts and design packages that are exclusively the Events by Pejublack aesthetic.
  • Build long-term relationships — our goal is not one event per client, but a lifetime of celebrations together.
  • Mentor and train emerging event professionals, giving back to the creative industry that shaped us.
  • Expand digital presence to attract international Nigerian diaspora clients planning weddings and celebrations in Lagos.
