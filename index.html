Who We Are

Lagos' Most Trusted
Event House

EVENTS BY PEJUBLACK is a Full-Service Event Planning, Design, and Management Company that specializes in creating exciting memories for our clients and their occasions.

We are deeply passionate about events and take great pride in delivering experiences that go beyond expectations, ensuring you always receive the best value for your money. From concept to execution, we transform visions into unforgettable experiences — making your celebration truly yours.

Creativity Excellence Integrity Passion Professionalism Trust Teamwork Attention to Detail

Every detail matters. We don't just plan events — we craft experiences that live forever in the hearts of those who attend.
